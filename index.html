StarPO (State‚ÄìThinking‚ÄìAction‚ÄìReward Policy Optimization)

StarPO is a multi-turn RL framework for optimizing entire interaction trajectories for LLM agents, with rollout and update stages interleaved.

We formulate agent-environment interactions as MDPs where states and actions are token sequences, enabling LLMs to reason over environment dynamics.

State: s_t, Action: a_t, Transition: $$P(s_{t+1} | s_t, a_t)$$

StarPO alternates between rollout and update. It supports PPO/GRPO-style objectives over multi-turn trajectories.
